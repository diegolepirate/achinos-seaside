Our Story

Where the Aegean becomes your table

Nestled directly on the golden sands of Analipsi beach, Achinos is more than a restaurant — it is an invitation to experience the Aegean with all your senses. Yannis and his dedicated team welcome you with the warmth of Cretan hospitality, serving the freshest catch alongside time-honored Mediterranean recipes.
